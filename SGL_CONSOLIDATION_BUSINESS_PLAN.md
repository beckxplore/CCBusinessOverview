### 3.1 Tiered Incentive Structure

We propose a performance-based incentive model that rewards leaders based on volume and performance:

#### **Tier 1: Bronze (Entry Level)**
- **Threshold:** 100-500 orders/month
- **Base Rate:** 12 ETB per order
- **Volume Bonus:** 0.50 ETB per kg
- **Example:** 300 orders, 800 kg = 4,000 ETB/month

#### **Tier 2: Silver (Growing Leader)**
- **Threshold:** 500-1,500 orders/month
- **Base Rate:** 15 ETB per order
- **Volume Bonus:** 0.75 ETB per kg
- **Example:** 800 orders, 2,500 kg = 13,875 ETB/month

- **Threshold:** 1,500+ orders/month
- **Base Rate:** 18 ETB per order
